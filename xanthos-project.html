...

Xanthos Digital, established in 2002 and based in Ely, Cambridgeshire, is a dedicated digital marketing agency committed to supporting small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) in achieving measurable growth and maximising return on investment.

Their comprehensive range of services includes digital strategy development, SEO, PPC management, email marketing, web design, and e-commerce solutions. These services are expertly tailored to enhance clients' online presence and drive sustainable success.

During my tenure as a Senior Designer at Xanthos Digital, I was responsible for leading client meetings to understand their unique needs and objectives, translating these into well-defined design briefs. I managed a small team of content and web designers, overseeing the entire design and development process from wireframing and prototypingâ€”using tools like Axureâ€”through to final implementation. Additionally, I gathered post-development feedback to inform sales strategies and ensure continuous improvement.

A significant aspect of my role involved designing book covers for best-selling IT publications, a task that required distilling complex, abstract technological concepts into captivating visual narratives. Furthermore, I developed branding and digital print materials for both B2B and B2C clients, maintaining consistency across all platforms. My expertise extended to creating responsive websites following usability best practices, utilising content management systems such as Kentico and WordPress to deliver user-friendly experiences.

My design process encompassed several key phases:

  1. Discovery and Planning: Defining business goals, target audiences, and core functionalities; conducting competitor research within the industry; and selecting the most suitable e-commerce platforms.
  2. Design and UX/UI: Developing wireframes to map out the site structure, establishing a visual style consistent with brand identity, and designing intuitive layouts to facilitate seamless navigation.
  3. Development and Integration: Implementing both front- and back-end development to bring designs to life, integrating essential tools, and ensuring robust functionality.
  4. Content Creation and Management: Entering product details, optimising content for search engines, and implementing content management systems to enable straightforward updates.
  5. Testing and Quality Assurance: Ensuring cross-browser and device compatibility, conducting thorough security audits, and adding SSL encryption to protect customer data.
  6. Launch and Deployment: Performing comprehensive final reviews, configuring hosting and domain settings, migrating sites to live environments, and monitoring performance to promptly address any initial issues.
  7. Ongoing Maintenance and Optimisation: Keeping plugins, platforms, and security features current, monitoring key performance metrics such as page load speed and conversion rates, and regularly updating product information.
