About Estela

With over a decade of experience in UX Design, I have cultivated a passion for creating accessible, engaging user experiences that not only meet user needs but also inspire and delight.

What drives me in my profession is the opportunity to develop products that are useful, usable, and desirable and empower people to effortlessly accomplish vital tasks.

Profile image of Estela Gaspar

In my roles as a UX and UI Designer, I focus on creating intuitive user journeys and UX flows, translating research insights into meaningful interfaces. I prototype in HTML and use GitHub for version control and collaboration. My process is rooted in data analysis, empathy mapping, and user-first thinking to ensure every decision supports real-world needs and behaviours.

Throughout my career, I have collaborated with various governmental organizations, including the Royal Navy, HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC), and the Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs (DEFRA). My goal has always been to design positive user experiences that benefit society. I am constantly honing my skills through ongoing training, including courses from Eviden, Norman Nielsen Group, and most recently, DataCamp. These opportunities keep me at the forefront of user-centered design (UCD) and ensure I am always evolving as a professional.

Professional Expertise

I have a broad range of experience encompassing UX Design, UI Design, User Research, Product Design, and Service Design. I am driven by a passion for continuous learning, which has led me to expand my skills into emerging areas like Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Python programming.

My work is grounded in Design Thinking, Lean, and Agile methodologies, and I adhere to the UK Government Digital Service (GDS) guidelines. I utilize design systems such as the GOV Design System and Google Material Design to create high-quality, scalable solutions. Currently, I am working toward earning my Six Sigma Green Belt certification.
