A type D producer is an organisation that carries out at least one of the following activities via their online marketplace:

  • facilitates the sale of filled packaging within the UK from non-UK producers
  • facilitates the sale of large quantities of unfilled packaging within the UK to non-obligated producers

Non-obligated producers are organisations that either:

  • produce less than 25 tonnes of packaging waste a year
