Report takeaway cups data

As a take-back scheme you need to create an account to help organisations report takeaway cups data.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Enviroment Agency

Published: 1 June 2023

To create an account you’ll need an email address and password. Then you’ll also need your:

  • approval code (this was emailed to you when you applied to be a take-back scheme)
  • companies house number
  • role within the organisation
  • organisation address
  • trading name
  • UK telephone number

You’ll also need to confirm what your organisation is registered as.

After you’ve created an account, you’ll be able to:

  • register your members in every nation where they have premises once per year
  • submit cups data on behalf of your members twice per year

You must keep any data you collect for 7 years.
