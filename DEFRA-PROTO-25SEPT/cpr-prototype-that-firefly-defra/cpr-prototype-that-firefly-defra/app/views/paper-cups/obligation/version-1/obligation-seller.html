Report takeaway cups recycling

If your organisation has premises in the UK and sells drinks in takeaway cups, you may need to create an account to collect and report data on them. This comes under your extended producer responsibility (EPR).

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Enviroment Agency

Published: 1 June 2023

Contents

  • When you do not need to create an account
  • When you do need to create an account
  • Before you start

When you do not need to create an account

You do not need to create an account if you’re:

  • a registered charity
  • a franchise - your organisation as a whole will report data for selling

When you do need to create an account

You need to create an account if all of the following apply:

  • you're an organisation who fills drinks in takeaway cups and sells them
  • you have premises in the UK, which includes anywhere you sell those drinks
  • you employ 10 or more full time or equivalent staff during a whole calendar year

This includes part time and seasonal employees whose total hours still average out as full time between January to the end of December.

Before you create an account

To create an account you’ll need your:

  • companies house number
  • role within the organisation
  • organisation address
  • trading name
  • UK telephone number

You’ll also need to confirm what your organisation is registered as.
