Guidance Report packaging data

Sign in or create an account for your organisation to report packaging data. This means it can comply with extended producer responsibility (EPR) for packaging.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Enviroment Agency

Published: Published 28 May 2021

Under EPR for packaging, organisations that supply packaging may need to report their packaging data. To do this, you need to create an account.

Compliance schemes also need to create an account.

If you already have one, sign in to your account.

You can use your account to:

  • register your organisation
  • report packaging data
  • tell us if you’re working with a compliance scheme

Who can create an account

You can create an account to report packaging data if your organisation:

  • supplies packaging to the UK market
  • imports, then empties and discards packaging in the UK
  • is a third party, such as a compliance scheme, that works with organisations that supply packaging

To create an account for an organisation, you must be one of the following:

  • director or company secretary
  • company partner
  • sole trader

Once you’ve created an account, you’ll be able to invite other people. You can give permission for another person to do things on your behalf within the account.
