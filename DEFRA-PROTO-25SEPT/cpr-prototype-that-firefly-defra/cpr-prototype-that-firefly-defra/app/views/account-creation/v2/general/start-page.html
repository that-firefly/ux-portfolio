Guidance Create an account to report packaging data

Create an account for your organisation to report its packaging data. This means it can comply with extended producer responsibility (EPR) for packaging.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Enviroment Agency

Published: Published 28 May 2021

Under EPR for packaging, organisations that handle and supply packaging may need to report their packaging data. To do this, you need to create an account.

You can use your account to:

  • register with the EPR for packaging scheme administrator
  • report packaging data
  • find out what fees you’ll need to pay

If you already have one sign in to your account.

Who can create an account

You can create an account to report packaging data if you’re:

  • an organisation that handles and supplies packaging
  • a third party, such as a compliance scheme, that works with organisations that handle and supply packaging

To create an account, you must be one of the following:

  • director or company secretary
  • company partner
  • sole trader

Once you’ve created your account, you’ll be able to invite other members. You can also give permission for a member to do things on your behalf within the account.

Before you start

To create an account to report your packaging data, you also need a GOV.UK account. This to confirm your identity.

If you do not have one, we’ll help you create a GOV.UK account first and then create your packaging data account.

You need to know:

  • your organisation’s details, for example address and phone number
  • your Companies House number, if you have one
