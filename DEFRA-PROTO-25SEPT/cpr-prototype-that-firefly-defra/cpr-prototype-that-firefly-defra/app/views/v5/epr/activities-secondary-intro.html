Did your organisation place any secondary packaging on the UK market in 2022?

Secondary packaging is packaging conceived to group a certain number of sales units whether sold to the final user or consumer or a means to stock shelves at the point of sale. It can be removed from the product without affecting its characteristics.

The secondary packaging is the cardboard trays and the plastic wrap.
