Accept or decline this request

How to give someone access to a service

  • If you're already registered with the service, you'll be able to give access to {!Custom:UserFullName;}
  • If you're not registered with the service you can register yourself. When your service registration is approved, you’ll be able to give {!Custom:UserFullName;} access to the service
  • If there’s another administrator within {!Organisation:Name;} who uses the service, they’ll be able to give access
  • If there are no administrators within {!Organisation:Name;} who use the service, you can give administrator access to {!Custom:UserFullName;} and they’ll be able to register themselves for the service

You have 30 days to action this request before {!Custom:UserFullName;} is removed from your records.
