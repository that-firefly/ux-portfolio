Check if your business needs to register

The collection and packaging reforms include two schemes, extended producer responsibility (EPR) and the deposit return scheme (DRS). Businesses may need to register for one or both schemes.

EPR scheme criteria

Your business must register for the EPR scheme if both of the following criteria apply:

  • it had a turnover above £1 million in its last financial year
  • it produced or placed 25 or more tonnes of packaging on the UK market between 1 January 2022 and 31 December 2022

DRS scheme criteria

Your business must register for the DRS scheme if it carries out at least one of these activities:

  • fills drinks containers with its own branded drinks to place on the market in England, Wales or Northern Ireland
  • imports filled drinks containers from outside of the UK to place on the market in England, Wales or Northern Ireland
  • sells filled drinks containers to end consumers in England, Wales or Northern Ireland
If you’re not sure, you can check if your business needs to register for the schemes.
