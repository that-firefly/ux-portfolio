What do I report, and how do ‘data periods’ work?

You need to report data that shows Acme Org's packaging activities over a period of time. Each period of time is called a ‘data period’.

For each data period Acme Org needs to:

  • report organisation details
  • report packaging data
  • pay the waste management fee

You may need to collect some information before the period starts, and you submit the data after the period has finished. This means you’ll have tasks for more than one data period at the same time.

You’ll also need to handle packaging recovery notes (PRNs). These are evidence that packaging waste has been recovered, reprocessed or exported. PRNs cover all packaging activities in a calendar year - they are not linked to specific data periods.
