Recycling obligations for packaging producers

Obligated packaging producers must cover the costs of recycling any packaging they place on the UK market.

Report your organisation’s packaging data

To register your organisation, you'll have to provide information like your turnover, contact details and packaging activities.

You do not need to use a compliance scheme to register or report your data.

Register for the first time

Register as a packaging producer.

You only need to register once. However, if you’re obligated, you’ll need to do the following annually:

  • pay the fees
  • report your packaging data
