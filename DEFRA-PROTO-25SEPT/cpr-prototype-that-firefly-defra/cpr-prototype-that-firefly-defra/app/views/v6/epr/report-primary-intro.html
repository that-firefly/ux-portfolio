Report your organisation's primary packaging for 2022

Primary packaging is what goods are contained in when sold to the end consumer. It’s sometimes called a ‘sales unit’.

The primary packaging is each individual crisp packet.

Example B

A crisp manufacturer sells large cardboard boxes containing individual packets of crisps to a wholesaler. The wholesaler sells full boxes of individual packets of crisps.
