What this means for your organisation

{% if data["small-producer"] == 'no' %}

You must:

  • record data about empty packaging and packaged goods you handle and supply in the UK market from 1 January 2023
  • create an account and register your organisation from July 2023
  • pay a fee to the environmental regulator
  • submit data about empty packaging and packaged goods you handled or supplied through the UK market

For the period January to June 2023, submit data between 1 July 2023 and 1 October 2023.

For the period July to December 2023, submit data between 1 January 2024 and 1 April 2024.

For any packaging that you handle and supply that is collected by local authorities from households or street bins, from April 2024 you must pay the scheme administrator:

  • a fee
  • a waste management fee

Meet your recycling obligations

You must buy packaging waste recyling notes (PRNs) or packaging waste export recycling notes (PERNs) in 2024.

We’ll let you know how many PRNs and PERNs you need to buy. We’ll base this on your 2023 data.

What are PRNs and PERNs?

A PRN or PERN is a certificate prooving that packaging waste has been recycled properly.

You can buy PRNs and PERNs from accredited reprocessors. Reprocessors are responsible for recycling packaging waste.

By buying PRNs and PERNs, you work towards meeting your recylcing target.

Submit nation data

You’ll need to submit nation data.

Nation data is information about which country in the UK your packaging has been sold, hired, loaned, gifted or discarded.

You must submit your nation data for the 2023 calendar year by 1 October 2024.
