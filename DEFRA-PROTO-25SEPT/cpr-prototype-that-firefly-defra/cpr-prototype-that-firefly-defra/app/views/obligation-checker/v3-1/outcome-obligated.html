Meet your recycling obligations

You must buy packaging waste recyling notes (PRNs) or packaging waste export recycling notes (PERNs) in 2024.

We’ll let you know how many PRNs and PERNs you need to buy. We’ll base this on your 2023 data.

What are PRNs and PERNs?

A PRN or PERN is a certificate prooving that packaging waste has been recycled properly.

You can buy PRNs and PERNs from accredited reprocessors. Reprocessors are responsible for recycling packaging waste.

By buying PRNs and PERNs, you work towards meeting your recylcing target.

Check if you need to submit nation data

You may need to submit nation data. This is information about which country in the UK your packaging has been sold, hired, loaned, gifted or discarded in.

You’ll need to submit your nation data for the 2023 calendar year by December 2024.

Who needs to submit nation data?

You’ll need to submit nation data if you:

  • sell filled or empty packaging directly to consumers in the UK
  • sell empty packaging to UK organisations that are not legally obligated under the regulations
  • hire or loan out reusable packaging
  • own an online marketplace that allows other organisations to sell their empty packaging and packaged goods to UK consumers
  • import packaging to the UK that you discard without selling or exporting it

Your next steps

  1. Prepare to collect data about the packaging you handle or supply in 2023. Find out more about the data you'll need to collect.
  2. Create an account on behalf on your organisation. Once you have created an account, you’ll receive email updates about the registration process and when it opens.
