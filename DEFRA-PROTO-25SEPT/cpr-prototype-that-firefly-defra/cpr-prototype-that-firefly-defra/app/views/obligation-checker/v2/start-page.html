Check if the extended producer responsibility for packaging regulations apply to you

Organisations that handle, supply, distribute or import packaging may be required to pay the full net cost of the disposal of their packaging, under new regulations.

Use this tool to find out:

  • if the regulations apply to you
  • what charges your organisation may need to pay
  • what you need to do next to comply with the new regulations

You'll need to know:

  • your annual turnover
  • the ways that your organisation handles or supplies packaging
  • the estimated total weight (in tonnes) of packaging you handle each calendar year
