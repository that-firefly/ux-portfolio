About wild pop animal portraits collection

This collection channels Warhol’s vibrant energy into your home.

The collection brings together the charm of a dog, the elegance of a cat, and the fierce majesty of a lion, all reimagined through an Andy Warhol lens.

Modern spaces, bold companions

Perfect for modern spaces, the designs add character to kitchens, brighten up hallways, and bring warmth to the cozy corners where pets love to rest.

Each image can also be transformed into striking neon light art with acrylic prints, creating a vibrant, contemporary statement piece that brings playful energy to any room.
