ElectroFlow Circuit

Just like blood redirects to an injured area, the ElectroFlow Circuit reroutes stored energy through its self-regulating microgrid. The system pulls reserve power from three key sources: kinetic energy (generated by vehicles and pedestrian movement), solar panels (embedded in road dividers and rooftops) and residual charge from EVs decelerating or braking.
