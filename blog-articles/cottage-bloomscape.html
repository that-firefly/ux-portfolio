Cottage Bloomscape
(lampshade and fabric design)

Drawwilder Drawwilder Pinterest

Cottage Bloomscape (Dusk and Morning Edition) is a collection of indoor lampshades created from my original artwork, inspired by garden dreamscapes and whimsical nostalgia.

Soft, layered florals and dusky tones evoke the quiet magic of evening light

Each piece tells a story of slow moments, blooming gardens, and a gentle return to simplicity, designed to bring warmth, character, and a poetic atmosphere into your home.

Each lampshade is thoughtfully designed and handcrafted using my original artwork

I offer a range of sizes, from smaller round shades (20cmØ x 18.5cm) to statement pieces (40cmØ x 25cm), as well as elegant oval designs (30 x 22 x 20cm), suitable for both ceiling and table lamps, including USA harp finial fittings.

You can choose from my existing designs or collaborate with me to create something entirely personal, with guidance available if you would like help deciding.

All pieces are printed on high-quality fabrics, including a beautiful linen-look rustic fabric and luxurious velvet.
